Handshake the Devil: Website Fingerprinting after HTTP/2

March, 2021

I know so much more about wikipedia now than I ever meant to learn. I've probably forgotten more about wikipedia's inner workings than most of its power-users and top contributors. Over the past few years, I've spent a lot of moonlighting hours decompressing from physics, stuck in bus stations, frustrated at other projects and taking out that frustration on what this project could have been – only to find that every question I and my collaborator had seeked to ask had been answered more than a year before we'd begun working on it.

It all started in the Winter of 2016.
