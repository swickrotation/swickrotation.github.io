About

Hey there, thanks for checking out my site. My name's Will, I'm a student of mathematical physics in the faculty of science at the University of Waterloo. My primary academic interests are in cosmology, quantum information and quantum computation, though I am dabbling and have done some small work in cryptography and security.

Outside of physics and mathematics, I'm also passionately political and have been an active member of numerous social justice initiatives over the years. At the moment I'm taking a small hiatus from that work to lead the Undergraduate Physics Society of the University of Waterloo (PhysClub).

I'm also an avid reader of literature and imbiber of visual and auditory art. Particular favourites in visual art are Toulouse-Lautrec, Edvard Munch, M.C. Escher, and Amedeo Modigliani. As for music, I place Miles above all else, and then a selection of other favourites are Bon Iver, Frank Ocean, Hey Rosetta!, Kendrick Lamar, Radiohead, Beethoven, Dead Kennedys, and recently LCD Soundsystem. Literature's a bit too mean a question to ask me to rank, but the book I've enjoyed the most this year has been GEB.
